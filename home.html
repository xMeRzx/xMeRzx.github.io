What is BIONICLE?

BIONICLE was a LEGO technic line that lasted from 2001 to 2010. It captured the hearts of fans of all ages from all over the world. To this day, the community is still alive and well although the line itself is discontinued. The community flourishes thanks to the contributors who have never given up on the franchise. This website is a tribute to the epic stories that shaped our childhoods.

What's this about?

This website is part of an ICT project. The purpose of this website is to introduce new fans to the franchise and explain the complex lore and content of the BIONICLE universe without spoilers.
