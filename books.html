Since 2003 books have been the central part of story telling in BIONICLE. Unlike movies or video games, the books give a very detailed picture of the BIONICLE world, lore and characters.

Serials

Serials surfaced in 2007 as yet another form of BIONICLE media. They are short stories slightly seperate from the main storyline, yet they piece together different events from the books and comics, for example. They are often written from the perspective of a side character.

Luckily reading the serials is not as big of a hassle as the books, as the former can be found here.
