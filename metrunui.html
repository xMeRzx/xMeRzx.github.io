Metru Nui

Metru Nui (literally translated as "Great City") was a city located under the island of Metru Nui. It was known as the City of Legends and can be considered the most central location in the Matoran Universe. It functioned as the brain of the Great Spirit, and the working Matoran who lived there were essential in keeping the universe intact and active.

The city is slightly smaller than the island of Mata Nui, about 2 145 square kilometres.
