The Skakdi are a short-tempered race native to the island of Zakaz. They started as a rather peaceful race, but became violent and vicious under the experiments of their home islands Makuta.

The Gang's on the loose

The most notable Skakdi would be the Piraka; seven Skakdi who - for a time - worked for the Dark Hunters. The name Piraka is Matoran for "thief and murderer", an accurate depiction for the group.
